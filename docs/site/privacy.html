Plume Privacy Policy

Plume sends data only to the Micropub endpoints you explicitly connect.

What Plume stores locally

  • Account tokens (IndieAuth OAuth)
  • Drafts (per blog and target URL)
  • Retry queue for failed posts
  • Cached server config (refreshed every 24h)

What Plume does NOT do

  • No analytics, telemetry, error reporting
  • No third-party services
  • No cross-device sync
  • The client_id page does not receive user data

Full policy + source: github.com/rmdes/plume
